Frequently Asked Questions

1) How do I register for an account?

To register with DB chat is very simple. Just go to the register icon to create your account in 7 easy steps. After you have registered then you will be able to login and enjoy the chat experience.

2) How do I use the chat?

To chat, first you have to login user your username and password. Then you will see an option in the main menu to enter chatroom. There you can send messages to your contacts.

3) Will DB chat keep a log of my messages? Also for how long?

Yes, your message history will be saved for 3 days.

4) How do I see my contacts?

To view your contact list, simply select the icon in the main menu "Contact List" to see all your friends. There is also a search field to help you locate your contacts quickly.

5) How do I view a contact profile?

To view a contact profile, simply select the contact name from your contact list to view his/her profile information.

6) How do I change my password?

To change your password, from the main menu select "View Profile" then "Change Password".
